Count subarrays/substrings with exactly K
👉 solved using
exact(K) = atMost(K) - atMost(K-1)

When this trick applies
--------------------------
Use it when:
Elements are non-negative OR
Problem is about distinct count / frequency
Sliding window can maintain a monotonic condition

5 Must-Know Problems
---------------------
1. Binary Subarrays With Sum k
2. Subarrays with K Different Integers
3. Count Number of Nice Subarrays
4. Longest Substring with At Most K Distinct Characters
5. Substrings with Exactly K Distinct Characters
