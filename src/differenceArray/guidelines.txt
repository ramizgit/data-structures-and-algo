General rule

Ask yourself these two questions:

1. Is the operation additive?

Examples:

+passengers
+bookings
+meetings
+brightness
+coverage

If yes, difference arrays are a candidate.

2. Is the coordinate range reasonably small?

If yes:

Difference Array

Otherwise:

Heap
Sweep Line
TreeMap
